About Us

21+ years of technical recruiting excellence.

SortIQ was founded on a simple premise: the best recruiting happens when recruiters have convesations about roles they truly understand and enjoy. Every team is different which makes getting to know the people on the team and the people we place our top priority.

Our team has advised and placed people at all levels for all kinds of software centric companies nationally since 2004. Startups in Angel, Seed, Series A-D, M&A, and pre-IPO stages. Government contractors with 0 through their 100th hire after winning their first prime contract. Growth and late stage software companies, and major companies that you have heard of.
