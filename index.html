Recruiter Assisted AI

After 17+ years of placing developers with the help of software with built in AI, we are turning the page and introducing a modernized approach: Recruiter Assisted AI.

What is SortIQ

SortIQ's AI-first recruiting firm platform prioritizes great conversations over the usual workflow grind so you can have more conversations with the sort of people you want to work with. Expect a fast, fluid, conversation funnel focused on elevating the people you want to hire until you hire them.

Why Try SortIQ

We firmly believe recruiting is mostly a get out of your own way problem so we pull all the stops and take you straight to recruiting the sort of people you want to hire the first day. The immediate results speak for themselves - you get a list of developers that you can start recruiting within minutes. You choose whether to do your own outreach to developers and initial screening or have a human from our Recruiter Assisted AI team do the yapping for you.
